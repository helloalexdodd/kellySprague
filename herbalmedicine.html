What is Herbalism?

herbalism or herbal medicine is the use of plants to treat illness or imbalance and support full body health. herbalism focuses on the root of the issue, rather than simply treating the symptoms. by treating the root cause, herbs can help the body heal itself and maintain long term health, especially when used in conjunction with diet and/or lifestyle support.

What does Herbalism treat?

herbal medicine can be used into a wide variety issues including digestion, hormones, fatigue, headaches, and anxiety - just to name a few! herbs can be beneficial in both chronic and acute health conditions.

what is an appointment like?

we will go through a full health history intake, covering any current or past health concerns and patterns. i will provide a custom plan that may include lifestyle, diet, and a herbal formula. the herbal formula is blended specifically for you - each herb is chosen for its potential to work specifically with your body, and in combination with the other herbs in your formula (wow!).
